# Design System: 06-country-explorer (ExploGeo)

## Visual Philosophy
The design follows a **Professional Dashboard** aesthetic, characterized by a clean white-space layout, indigo accents, and immersive background glow effects in the hero section.

## Design Elements
-   **Hero Section**: High-contrast dark header with radial blurs for depth.
-   **Typography**: Bold tracking for brand elements; clean sans-serif for dashboard data.
-   **Grid Cards**: Rounded editorial-style cards (3xl-4xl) for country data.

## Color Palette
-   **Primary Accent**: Indigo-600 (#4f46e5)
-   **Secondary Accent**: Blue-500 (#3b82f6)
-   **Dashboard Base**: Slate Gray (#f8fafc)
-   **Typography**: Deep Slate (#0f172a)

## Interaction Design
-   **Dynamic Filtering**: Real-time search and region selection.
-   **Modal Transitions**: Smooth backdrop-blurred overlays for detailed country data.
-   **Hover Micro-interactions**: Smooth card lifting and icon color shifts.
